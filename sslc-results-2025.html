SSLC 2025: Tulu Language Achieves 100% Pass Rate Across Coastal Blocks

THE 100% GENERATION: Students who have mastered the Tulu language track in the 2025 SSLC examinations.

The latest data from the 2025 SSLC examinations marks a historic milestone for Tuluva education. Across the educational blocks of Udupi and Dakshina Kannada, students who opted for Tulu as a language subject have achieved a perfect 100% pass rate.

THE DATA GAP: While the community delivers 100% results, the political effort toward Article 345 recognition remains at 0%.

Key Findings:

  • Academic Advantage: The data proves that students find an academic advantage when learning in their mother tongue.
  • Regional Success: Performance was consistent across both urban and rural blocks in the Tulu-speaking belt.
  • The Challenge: Despite these results, several schools in areas like Kundapura still do not offer Tulu, forcing students into other language tracks.

This report serves as a formal documentation of our linguistic strength. We use this statistical evidence to demand that Tulu be treated not as an elective, but as a constitutional right for every student in Tulu Nadu.
